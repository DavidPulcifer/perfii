How the two savings accounts work

Accessible savings is money you want to keep easy to reach, such as an emergency reserve. Long-term savings is money you are setting aside for later.

While a goal's accessible balance is below its target, 100% of that goal goes to the accessible account. Once the target has been reached, future contributions go entirely to the long-term account. If you do not choose a long-term account, contributions always stay accessible.
